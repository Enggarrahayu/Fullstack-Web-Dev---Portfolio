API for Driver and Mechanic Mobile App

This project was developed for Lookmandjaja Logistics Company to seamlessly integrate the delivery and workshop processes between the company's website and mobile applications, ensuring continuous and efficient operations.

The workshop and warehouse management website, operated by administrators, is integrated with the mechanic mobile app, allowing for real-time updates and coordination. Similarly, the operational management website, also managed by administrators, is integrated with the driver mobile app to streamline delivery processes

Below is a brief overview of how the driver and mechanic mobile apps are integrated with the website.
