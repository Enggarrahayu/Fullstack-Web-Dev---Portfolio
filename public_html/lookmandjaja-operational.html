Lookmandjaja Operational Project

The Lookmandjaja Operational Project, developed for Lookmandjaja Logistics Company, tracks every step of the internal delivery process, from order shipment to final receipt by the customer.

The project is divided into two modules: Operational Carter and Operational Eceran.
Below is a brief overview of the Lookmandjaja Operational Project.

Operational Carter

In Operational Carter, each delivery order is dedicated to fulfilling a single customer's order.

Operational Eceran

In Operational Eceran, each delivery order is dedicated to fulfilling multiple customer's order.

Processing and Documentation of Incoming Orders (Create New Order Form)

This project was developed using Laravel Framework and was utilizing Jquery and Ajax to enhance the user experience and streamline interactions between the client and server.

Delivery Order Process of Operational Carter

In this process, users are required to input various fields, including vehicle details, co-driver information, costs, fuel vouchers, and cashier vouchers, to complete the form submission.



After the form submission, the backend is also responsible for integrating with the accounting module to track and report the fuel vouchers and cashier vouchers used for each delivery order.

Once the delivery order is created, the process continues through several stages until completion, including tracking the delivery progress in real-time via the driver's mobile app
